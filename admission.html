✶ Admission Procedure:
A student who desires to join the school, should obtain a copy of the prospectus from the office and fill up the prescribed form of admission attached therein He/She should affix recent copy of passport size photograph and will have to appear for interview before the Principal along with either of the parents or guardian with following documents.
1. Admission form duly completed in all respects.
2. Original statement of marks of S.S.C. Examination and a Xerox copy of the same duly attested.
3. School leaving Certificate and a Xerox of the same duly attested.
4. Three copies of passport size photograph.
5. Character certificates from the school last attended.
6. Copy of Aadhar Card.
7. Copy of Bank details (Account number, IFSC Code).
8. Copy of Caste certificate. (If any)
9. Income Certificate if the student is eligible for various scholarships.

    ✶ Eligibility for Admission:-
  • 1. Students passing S.S.C.(Std. X) Exam of the Goa Board of Secondary and Higher Secondary Education, Alto Betim or an equivalent examination of any other recognized Board are eligible for admission to Std. XI. Students passing Std. XI are eligible for admission to Std.XII.
  • 2.Students having passed S.S.C. (Std X) Exam of its equivalent Exam from Board other than Goa Board should necessarily submit Eligibility Certificate issued by the Goa Board and counter signature on LC, Migration Certificate. Results would be declared only after Eligibility Certificate is obtained.
  • 3.Admissions to any class will be as per the discretion of the Principal, subject to the rules of admission. No Admission will be confirmed without original Leaving Certificate.
  • 4.A student will be eligible to appear for Examination of Goa Board at the end of the second academic year provided he/she passes Std. XI on the average results of the Exam held during the first academic year, keeps two terms in the second academic year completes the prescribed course successfully with required attendance for regular classes, possesses a certificate of having completed the works satisfactorily in Physical Education, Env. Education /NSS/ Computer Awareness and attains the required standard in conduct and progress.
  • 5.No admission shall be regarded as duly granted unless it is granted by the authority of the Principal, the necessary fees are timely paid and the admission is approved by the Goa Board.

Refund of fees
Fees once paid will not be refunded.
Granting of terms.
Granting of terms will strictly depend upon the fulfilment of the following conditions to the satisfaction of the Principal and other teaching staff members.
i.Minimum attendance 75% of the days in which lectures are delivered (Absenting from even a single lecture/practical’s will be treated as absence for the whole day)
ii. Regular attendance at Tutorial/ Extra classes/school function/Day of National importance is essential.
iii. Good conduct and observance of discipline is a must.

Rules & regulations:
1. Every students must have his/her identity card while in the School premises.
2. Any misuse of or damage to school property is liable to fine, penalty and recovery.
3. All students are responsible to the school authorities for behaviour inside and / or outside the school premises.
4. Every student must appear for every test and/or Examination conducted by the school, otherwise they are not entitled for promotion/ for filling of forms for Board Examinations.
5. Absolute silence is expected in the school library.
6. Ragging, Smoking, Consumption of alcohol, vulgar and in decent dress and behaviour is strictly prohibited in the school premises.
7. Every notice issued and rules framed time to time by the Principal should be strictly followed.
8. Students must not loiter in the school premises while the classes are in progress.
9. If for any reason, in the opinion of the Principal, the continuance of a student is found to be detrimental to the best interests of the school the Principal may ask the student to leave the school without assigning any reason and will be final.
10. Collection of any kinds of funds, entry to visitors, attending classes other than own, organisation of any function or activity etc. are all subject to Principal's permission. Not our/picnic/hike outing can be arranged without Principal's permission/ approval.
11. Rules of Examination under malpractice Act will be strictly followed. Those violating these rules are liable to be expelled.

Rules & Regulations: 1.Mobiles are not allowed in School. If found mobile sets would be confiscated.
2.So also every student should observe proper dress (Uniform) code. neck-lines and waist should be decent and modest.
3.No fashionable haircuts are allowed
4.Every student should abide by these rules very strictly. Parents need to monitor these instructions and rules.
