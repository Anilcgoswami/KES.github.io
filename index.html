OUR VISION

Excellence in Education and the Holistic development of a student.
We strive to create an enabling environment providing relevant opportunities which equip the students with knowledge and skills as well as attitudes and values required to face life with courage and conviction. We believe human excellence is the aim of all education.

OUR MISSION

Providing an Environment conducive to Learning and Competency

  • Providing an Environment conducive to Learning and Competency
  • To create an enabling environment where students ‘learn to learn’
  • To instill in all students the ability to communicate freely and confidently.
  • To motivate each student to compete with himself to reach his/her true potential.
  • To equip our students with winning skills and train them to be competent as well as original.
  • To nurture young minds, strengthen their values and make them worthy human beings.

ABOUT US

The Kamakshi Education Society's Higher Secondary School,was established in June !992,with an aim of imparting sound and wholesome education for moral and intellectual development of the students, with a view to enable them to play a constructive role in the "Man making and Nation Building".

This Higher Secondary being located in a most convenient place aims to cater to the needs of the students of Ponda town as well as the neighbouring villages with a strong urge to provide personalised approach and attention in imparting knowledge and values,which otherwise was diminishing in the urban centres. More stress is given to provide value based education eeping in mind the needs of Society.The institution proudly celebrated the SILVER JUBILEE of it's establishment in the year 2017-18.
